# Code Of Conduct

RunnerMonitor is maintained as a practical engineering project. Contributors
are expected to keep discussion respectful, specific, and focused on making the
tool safer and more useful.

## Expected Behavior

- Be direct, but courteous.
- Assume good intent while asking for clear evidence.
- Keep reviews focused on code, documentation, tests, and operational safety.
- Respect privacy: do not expose local paths, hostnames, tokens, passwords, or
  other sensitive information from another person's environment.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing secrets or private infrastructure details.
- Pressuring maintainers to run destructive operations without explicit safety
  review.
- Repeatedly ignoring maintainer guidance after clarification.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, hide, or remove content and may restrict participation
when behavior risks the project, contributors, or private infrastructure.
